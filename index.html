Services

Ui/Ux Desing

I am able to design mechatronic systems based on the synthesis of mechanisms, determination and specification of analog electronic systems composed of passive, low power active and combinational or sequential digital logic systems; in addition, to differentiate techniques and algorithms for the analysis of systems pneumatic, hydraulic and thermal; likewise, to design control systems in continuous time through the modeling of equations differentials and their numerical simulation, as well as analyzing the chemical, physical and electromagnetic properties of materials. capable of developing mechatronic systems based on design tools and computer-aided manufacturing (CAD and CAM). In the same way, apply automation schemes through the selection of sensors and actuators, as well as the programming of electronic and electromechanical systems with a level of integrating mechanical, electronic, control and computing technologies, among which may include: geometric configurations of manipulator robots, communication elements, fault diagnosis and implementation in industrial networks and man-machine interfaces and the planning, management, monitoring and evaluation of projects for the development, updating and/or improvement of mechatronic systems.
