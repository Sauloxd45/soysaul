Ui/Ux Desing

I am able to design mechatronic systems based on the synthesis of mechanisms, determination and specification of analog electronic systems composed of passive, low power active and combinational or sequential digital logic systems; in addition, to differentiate techniques and algorithms for the analysis of systems pneumatic, hydraulic and thermal; likewise, to design control systems in continuous time through the modeling of equations differentials and their numerical simulation, as well as analyzing the chemical, physical and electromagnetic properties of materials. capable of developing mechatronic systems based on design tools and computer-aided manufacturing (CAD and CAM).
